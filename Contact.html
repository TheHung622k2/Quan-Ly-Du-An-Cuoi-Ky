frequently asked questions

How to cancel ?

You can cancel from the app by scroll through details to 'Cancel Reservation' link at the bottom and click 'OK'.

What are payment methods ?

Guests can pay for their stay at hotel with Runch Honors Points, Major credit / Debit card.

How to claim coupons codes ?

You can claim coupons codes on their app with more promotions.

What are the age requirements ?

You can click the 'Hotel details' link. Scroll to the bottom of the pop-up to click the 'Hotel website' link and expand the 'Additional hotel policies' section will be show and minimum age to register will be listed. You can also call the hotel directly to ask about minimum ages and identification needed.
